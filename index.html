Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new product?
Go to the Products page and click the "Add Product" button. Fill in the product details including name, description, price, and category. You can also upload images and set inventory levels.
How can I track my sales?
Use the Dashboard for real-time sales data or go to the Analytics page for detailed reports. You can filter by date range, product category, or customer segment.
How do I manage customer orders?
Navigate to the Orders page to view all customer orders. You can update order status, process refunds, and send shipping notifications from this page.
Can I export my data?
Yes, you can export product lists, order history, and financial reports in CSV or Excel format from their respective pages using the export buttons.
How do I set up promotions?
Edit any product and check the "Mark as Promotion" box. Set the discount percentage and promotion period. Promotional products will be highlighted on the customer-facing store.
